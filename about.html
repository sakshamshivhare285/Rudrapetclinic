About Us

At Rudra Pet Clinic, we understand that pets are not just animals – they are family. That’s why our mission is to provide compassionate, high-quality veterinary care to ensure the health and happiness of your beloved companions. Located in the heart of India, we are proud to offer a comprehensive range of services designed to cater to the specific needs of pets of all breeds, ages, and sizes. From routine wellness check-ups to complex surgeries, our clinic is equipped with state-of-the-art technology, and our team of skilled veterinarians is dedicated to delivering exceptional medical care. We are committed to offering not just treatments but a personalized, holistic approach to your pet’s well-being, focusing on both their physical and emotional health.

Our services include preventive care, which is the cornerstone of maintaining long-term health. We provide regular health check-ups, vaccinations, parasite control, and nutritional counseling to ensure that your pets are healthy and happy for years to come. For pets requiring advanced care, we offer comprehensive diagnostics using modern tools to accurately diagnose and treat a wide range of illnesses. Whether it’s surgery, dental care, or specialized treatments, we take great pride in using the latest medical techniques and ensuring that your pet is as comfortable as possible throughout their treatment journey. We believe that prevention is better than cure, which is why we emphasize educating pet owners on the importance of regular veterinary visits and proactive health management. At Rudra Pet Clinic, we treat every pet with the same love and attention we would give our own, and we are committed to building lasting relationships with our clients based on trust, care, and the highest standard of service.

We invite you to visit us and experience the Rudra Pet Clinic difference. Your pet’s health is our priority, and we are here to support you every step of the way.

Dr. V. M. Dhoot

Dr. V. M. Dhoot is a highly experienced veterinary professional with over 15+ years of expertise in Veterinary Medicine. He completed his undergraduate studies in 1991, followed by his post-graduation in 1993, and a post-doctoral degree in 2012. His passion for academics and research is evident through his remarkable contributions, including 120 research publications, 16 technical bulletins, and 25 popular articles. Dr. Dhoot has mentored numerous students, guiding nine as a Major Advisor, nineteen as a Minor Advisor, and one post-doctoral student.

His dedication to extension activities is highlighted by his authorship of eight books. Dr. Dhoot has also played a significant role in research, acting as Co-Principal Investigator for nine research projects with a total funding of over 6 crores. His academic journey has been supported by prestigious scholarships such as the ICAR and Dr. PDKV, Akola Merit Scholarship during his undergraduate and postgraduate studies.

Throughout his career, Dr. Dhoot has received numerous accolades and awards, including the ISVM Appreciation Award, ISVS Appreciation Award for Poster Session, Certificate of Commendation from the Department of Forest, Government of Maharashtra (2007, 2010, 2012, 2022), ISVM Certificate of Merit for Poster Session, the Indian Immunological Gold Medal Award, Dr. B. M. Arora Best Wildlife Veterinarian Scientist Award, Dr. P. K. Das Gold Medal, and many others. His work continues to inspire and contribute to the field of Veterinary Medicine.
